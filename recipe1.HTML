Fluffy Kenyan Mandazi

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 15 mins Makes: 20 pieces

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Add egg and coconut milk gradually
  3. Knead into smooth dough (5-7 minutes)
  4. Let rest covered for 30 minutes
  5. Roll out dough to 1/2 inch thickness
  6. Cut into triangles or desired shapes
  7. Heat oil to medium heat (350Â°F)
  8. Fry until golden brown on both sides
  9. Drain on paper towels and serve warm

Serving Tradition

Best with Kenyan tea | Breakfast favorite | Street food classic
