Authentic Kenyan Chicken Stew

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 45 mins Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 3 tomatoes, blended
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in pot, brown chicken pieces
  2. Remove chicken, saut√© onions until soft
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute
  4. Stir in tomato paste and blended tomatoes
  5. Add turmeric and chicken stock
  6. Return chicken to pot, bring to boil
  7. Reduce heat, simmer for 30 minutes
  8. Season with salt and pepper
  9. Serve hot with ugali or rice

Serving Tradition

Sunday family meal | Celebration dish | Comfort food
